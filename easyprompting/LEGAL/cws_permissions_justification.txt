Justification for the permissions required by the "Easy Prompting" extension:

**1. `windows`**
   - Justification: Used to open the prompt scratchpad in its own small window and to close it again when the toolbar icon is clicked a second time. A dedicated window (rather than a toolbar popup) lets the pad stay open next to the user's AI chat while they type and copy, which is the core purpose of the extension.

**2. `storage`**
   - Justification: Used to save the user's prompt drafts and preferences (such as text size) locally on their own device, so drafts are preserved across browser sessions and a stray keystroke can never lose them. No data is transmitted anywhere.

**3. `system.display`**
   - Justification: Used to read the screen dimensions so the pad window opens in a predictable position (bottom-right by default). This is the only use of this permission.
