Easy Prompting is a safe scratchpad — a prompt draft pad — for writing your AI chatbot prompts before you send them.

Many AI chat interfaces lose your in-progress prompt far too easily. A wrong key in a terminal or TUI (like Ctrl+C), or pressing Enter when you meant Shift+Enter, Alt+Enter or Ctrl+Enter, and the prompt you were carefully writing is gone — or sent too early. Easy Prompting fixes that: you write in a calm window that stays open beside your chat, and NOTHING is ever sent from it. Here, Enter just makes a new line. When the prompt is ready, one click copies it over.

Think of it as a prompt keeper / safe prompt notepad that sits next to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI agent with a chat box.

✍️ A SAFE PLACE TO WRITE
• Enter never sends — nothing leaves this window, so no keystroke can fire your draft early.
• Autosave — every keystroke is stored locally; close the window and your prompt is still there when you come back.
• Stays open — a real window beside your chat, not a popup that vanishes the moment you click away.

🗂️ JUGGLE SEVERAL PROMPTS
• Multiple tabs — keep a system prompt, a working draft and reusable snippets side by side.
• One-click Copy — send the current prompt to your clipboard, then paste it into any chat.
• Live word & character counter and adjustable text size.

⌨️ KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS (work while typing)
• Alt + E / Alt + R — previous / next tab
• Alt + 1…9 — jump straight to a tab
• Alt + ← / → or Ctrl + [ / ] — previous / next tab

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything is stored on your own device via chrome.storage and is never transmitted. No accounts, no tracking, no network requests.
